## Project Identity

Repository name: IX-PackHunt-Guard

Project purpose: a governed anti-abuse gateway research project intended to
record normalized intent fragments, correlate distributed request patterns,
identify capability assembly pressure, route risk through evidence-bound policy
controls, and export review packets for BlackFox, CognitionKernel, and Autonomy
Assurance workflows.

## Claim Boundary

IX-PackHunt-Guard does not claim to make jailbreaks impossible.

IX-PackHunt-Guard does not claim to prevent all AI abuse, certify model safety,
replace human review, or fix any hosted frontier model from the outside.

The project makes a narrower, testable claim: when AI traffic passes through this
gateway, the system can record normalized intent fragments, detect whether those
fragments are assembling into restricted capability patterns across time,
sessions, or principals, gate the response, and export evidence for human
review.
